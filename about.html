👩‍🍳
Meet Selina

Baking with Heart, Every Single Day

Selina Chimukangara has been perfecting her baking craft for over a decade, turning family recipes and personal passion into Maryland's most beloved home bakery. What started as weekend treats for friends and family quickly grew into something much bigger.

Every item at Selly Bake House is crafted from scratch using the finest ingredients — real butter, farm-fresh eggs, and premium chocolate. No shortcuts. No artificial flavors. Just pure, toothsome goodness the way Grandma used to make it.

Based in Rockville, MD, Selina bakes to order and delivers across Maryland, bringing joy one box at a time.
